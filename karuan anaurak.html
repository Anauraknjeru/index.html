Lemon Meringue Pie

Ingredients (makes one 9-inch pie):

  • 1 recipe pastry for a 9 inch double crust pie
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 6 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup cold water

Directions


ADD HEAT TO THE OVEN.

Blend sugar, cornstarch, and salt in pan. Steadily mix in water until combination is smooth.

Join continually, heat to the point of boiling over medium intensity. Cook until thick.

Eliminate from heat. Steadily mix around 1/4 cup hot combination into egg yolks; add back to hot blend, mixing constantly.

Mix in lemon juice. Empty filling into hull .

To Make Meringue:

Beat egg whites (at room temperature) until foamy. Add cream of tartar and MIX until stiff peaks form.

Spread meringue over filling, sealing to edge of crust.

Bake for 60 minutes in the preheated oven, or until meringue is golden brown.
